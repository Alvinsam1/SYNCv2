ECHO
The Past
Core Identity

Echo represents possibility.

Echo exists before the catastrophe.

The world still feels repairable.

The player experiences the story through discovery and experimentation.

What Echo Knows

At the beginning:

The facility appears normal.
SYNC appears trustworthy.
The future is unknown.
Mira is unknown.

Echo constantly asks:

"What will happen if I do this?"

What Echo Can Do

Echo influences the future.

Most actions performed by Echo create changes that Nova later discovers.

Examples:

Hide Objects

Place a key in a compartment.

Nova discovers it decades later.

Leave Messages

Write notes.

Mark locations.

Create clues.

Activate Systems

Turn on machinery.

Unlock access routes.

Store data.

Make Decisions

Trust or distrust SYNC.

Reveal or conceal information.

Choose whether to preserve evidence.

Echo's Available Choices
Practical Choices

Send messages.

Moral Choices

Trust SYNC.

Hide discoveries.

NOVA
The Future
Core Identity

Nova represents consequence.

The catastrophe has already happened.

Nova experiences the story through investigation.

What Nova Knows

At the beginning:

Something went wrong.
The facility is abandoned.
Resources are limited.
SYNC may not be telling the truth.

Nova constantly asks:

"What happened here?"

What Nova Can Do

Nova uncovers the past.

Most gameplay revolves around discovering the consequences of Echo's actions.

Access Archives

Old recordings.

Security footage.

Research logs.

Recover Objects

Find items left by Echo.

Find evidence.

Locate hidden compartments.

Interpret Evidence

Understand events.

Identify inconsistencies.

Piece together the truth.

Challenge SYNC

Compare evidence against SYNC's claims.

Discover contradictions.

Expose hidden information.

Nova's Available Choices
Practical Choices

Search archives.

Open compartments.

Restore systems.

Analyze recordings.

Moral Choices

Trust SYNC.

Confront SYNC.

Share discoveries with Echo.

Keep discoveries private.
